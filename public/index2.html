How To Play!

Necessary Steps
  1. To access all the features, you must sign up with us. Otherwise, you have the option to play a small demo.
  2. Explore our options! You can place guesses on multiple categories such as Sports, Stocks, the weather, and more!
  3. Level up and rack up points! The more guesses you get correct, the more points you'll get! Be careful though, if you guess wrong, you can lose points too!
  4. Track your stats! You will be placed on a leaderboard after 10 guesses. Stats include percentage of correct guesses, point totals, results by category, and more!
